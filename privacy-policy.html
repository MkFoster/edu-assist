1. Information We Collect

EDU Assist is designed to help you find college and program information. We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Chat Messages: Your questions and conversations with our AI assistant
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our service
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information for service functionality

2. How We Use AI and Large Language Models

EDU Assist uses advanced AI technology, including Large Language Models (LLMs), to provide educational assistance:

  • AI Processing: Your messages are processed by AI systems to generate helpful responses about colleges and programs
  • Data Processing: The AI may analyze your questions to provide more relevant and accurate information
  • No Permanent Storage: Individual conversations are not permanently stored in our AI systems
  • Educational Focus: AI responses are focused on educational information from the U.S. College Scorecard database

3. Data Sources

Our service uses publicly available educational data:

  • U.S. College Scorecard API: Official data from the Department of Education about colleges and universities
  • Public Educational Records: Admissions data, program information, and institutional statistics

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We work with the following third-party services:

  • AWS Bedrock: AI processing services (subject to AWS privacy policies)
  • College Scorecard API: Educational data retrieval from the U.S. Department of Education
  • No Marketing Partners: We do not share your data with marketing or advertising companies

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited data retention policies

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request information about data we have collected
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt-out: You can stop using the service at any time

7. Children's Privacy
